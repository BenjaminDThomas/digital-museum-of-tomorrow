AI & Trust

How we use AI — honestly, inclusively, and with accountability

These AI tools are designed to open new pathways into the V&A collection — not replace curatorial expertise. We are committed to transparency about what AI can and cannot do, who these tools serve, and how we mitigate their harms.

Our commitments

Six guiding principles

01

Transparency

Every AI-generated element is clearly labelled. We show you why an object was recommended, what data was used, and where our sources come from.

  • All recommendations show reasoning
  • Sources cited in chatbot responses
  • Similarity scores shown on visual search
  • Generative content clearly marked AI-created
02

Bias awareness

Museum collections reflect historical power structures. The V&A's holdings over-represent certain cultures, periods, and acquisition contexts. We acknowledge this openly.

  • Underrepresented collections deliberately surfaced
  • Bias notices shown throughout tools
  • Ongoing audit of recommendation diversity
  • Historical language flagged where found
03

Inclusion

These tools are designed for everyone — regardless of prior knowledge, disability, age, or cultural background.

  • Full keyboard navigation throughout
  • Screen reader compatible (ARIA, semantic HTML)
  • Large text and high contrast modes
  • Plain language throughout — no jargon
  • Preference controls stored locally only
04

Privacy

We do not track individuals, create persistent profiles, or share your data. Your preferences are stored in your browser only.

  • No user accounts or login required
  • No personal data sent to external servers
  • Interest preferences stored locally only
  • Chat history not retained after session
05

Accuracy & humility

AI can be wrong. We always include caveats, point back to authoritative sources, and never present AI output as definitive museum statements.

  • Chatbot responses cite V&A API data
  • AI interpretations framed as explorations
  • Links to authoritative V&A records
  • Clear error states and fallback messaging
06

Human centrality

AI is a guide, not a gatekeeper. You are always in control. These tools supplement — never replace — human curiosity, curatorial knowledge, and museum expertise.

  • You can always bypass AI tools entirely
  • Direct links to V&A collections provided
  • No filter bubbles — serendipity mode available
  • Feedback mechanism for AI errors
